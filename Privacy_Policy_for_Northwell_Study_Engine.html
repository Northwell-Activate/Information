This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes the manner in which Evidation Health, Inc. (“Evidation”, “we,” or “us”) and the mobile application provided by us (the “App”) use and collect data from you in connection with your participation in the study being conducted by The Feinstein Institutes for Medical Research (“FIMR”) under the protocol: Acceptability and Feasibility of Leveraging Person Generated Health Data (PGHD) to Uncover New Ways to Improve Northwell Patients’ Cardiology and Behavioral Health Outcomes, IRB # 22-0029 (the “Study”). This Policy describes the information that we gather from you on the App, how we use and disclose such information, and the steps we take to protect such information. The App is only intended for use in connection with the Study. Additional information about how we use and collect data is described in the Informed Consent Form (the “ICF”) provided to you in connection with the Study,. If you have not signed an ICF, then you may not use the App. In the event of a conflict between the terms of this Policy and the ICF, the ICF will control with respect to the conflict.


Information We Collect on the App:


  • User-provided Information. When you use the App, we may collect information about you that you actively provide or submit, including your name, email address, preferences, survey responses, and measurements you provide. You may provide us with information in various ways on the App. For example, you provide us with information when you register for an account or use the App.


  • Passively Collected Information. The App passively collects information, such as media access control (MAC) address, service set identifier (SSID), battery level, and your interactions with the App, including when the App is opened, and time spent on each screen of the App. This collection cannot be disabled.

    Separately, you can opt-in to the App’s collection of your movement patterns based on your device’s precise geolocation. If you opt-in, we will also collect the time and distance you travel, but never your actual location. If you opt-in and later wish to opt-out, you may prevent your device from sharing precise location information at any time through your device’s operating system settings.


  • Information from Other Sources. We may obtain information from third parties, including any third party service providers or partners, and sources other

    than the App, such as from your study coordinator and any wearable device you have connected to the App.


  • Communications. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you. For example, when you contact us, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, the contents of a message or attachments that you may send to us, and other information you choose to provide.


    How We Use the Information We Collect. We will only use the information we collect on the App for two purposes. Specifically, we use information we collect on the App to provide and maintain the App and in connection with the Study as follows:


  • We use information that we collect on the App in accordance with the ICF, which describes the Study, including any data collection and use as part of the Study.


  • We use the information that we collect on the App to operate, maintain, enhance and provide all features of the App.


  • We may use your email address or other information we collect to contact you for administrative purposes such as to send communications, or to help you recover your account.


  • We use the information that we collect to communicate with you, provide you with updates and other information relating to the App, provide information that you request, respond to comments and questions, and otherwise provide App support.


    When We Disclose Information. Except as described and/or as you permit in the ICF, we will not disclose information about you that we collect on the App to third parties without your written consent. We may disclose information to third parties if you consent to us doing so, as well as in the following circumstances:


  • We work with third party service providers to provide website, application development, hosting, maintenance, and other services for us. These third parties may have access to or process information about you as part of providing those services for us. We limit the information provided to these service providers

    to that which is necessary for them to perform their services, and we require them to agree to maintain the confidentiality of such information.


  • We may disclose information about you if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws, in response to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena or warrant, or to otherwise cooperate with law enforcement or other governmental agencies.


  • We also reserve the right to disclose information about you that we believe, in good faith, is appropriate or necessary to: (i) take precautions against liability; (ii) protect ourselves or others from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful uses or activity;

(iii) investigate and defend ourselves against any third-party claims or allegations; (iv) protect the security or integrity of the App and any facilities or equipment used to make the App available; or (v) protect our property or other legal rights (including, but not limited to, enforcement of our agreements), or the rights, property, or safety of others. However, we will in these instances take reasonable efforts to maintain the confidentiality of your personal information to the extent possible.


Your Choices


You may, of course, decline to share certain information with us, in which case we may not be able to provide to you some of the features and functionality of the App.


Subject to any limitations related to your participation in the study, you may update, correct, or delete your account information and preferences at any time by contacting your FIMR study coordinator. If you wish to access or amend any other personal information we hold about you, you may contact your FIMR study coordinator. Deletion of your account will not necessarily unenroll you from the study. If you would like to unenroll from the FIMR study, you will need to follow the process outlined in the ICF and/or contact your FIMR study coordinator.


Please note that while any changes you make will be reflected in active user databases within a reasonable period of time, we may retain information you submit for backups, archiving, prevention of fraud and abuse, analytics,

satisfaction of legal obligations, or where we otherwise reasonably believe that we have a legitimate reason to do so.


If you opt-in to the App’s collection of your location information, you can prevent your device from sharing precise location information with the App at any time through your device’s operating system settings.


Third-Party Apps


The App may contain features or links to websites, apps and services provided by third parties. Any information you provide on third-party sites, apps or services is provided directly to the operators of such services and is subject to those operators’ policies, if any, governing privacy and security, even if accessed through the App. We are not responsible for the content or privacy and security practices and policies of third-party sites, apps or services to which links or access are provided through the App. We encourage you to learn about third parties’ privacy and security policies before providing them with information.


Data Security


We use certain physical, managerial, and technical safeguards that are designed to ensure the integrity and security of information that we collect and maintain.

Please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We cannot and do not guarantee that information about you will not be accessed, viewed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.


International Visitors


The App is hosted in the United States and is intended for visitors located within the United States. If you choose to use the App from the European Union or other regions of the world with laws governing data collection and use that may differ from U.S. law, then please note that you are transferring your personal information outside of those regions to the U.S. for storage and processing. Also, we may transfer your data from the U.S. to other countries or regions in connection with storage and processing of data, fulfilling your requests, and operating the App. By providing any information, including personal information, on or to the App, you consent to such transfer, storage, and processing.
